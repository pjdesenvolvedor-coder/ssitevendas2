# **App Name**: StreamPulsar

## Core Features:

- Public Product Listing: Display a catalog of available streaming service accounts (e.g., Netflix, Disney+, Max) with key details like service name, features, and pricing on the public-facing site.
- Guest Checkout Process: Allow customers to select and purchase streaming accounts through a simplified, secure checkout flow that does not require pre-registration or login.
- Admin Product Management: Provide administrators with a dedicated interface to add new streaming service accounts, modify existing product details, update pricing, and manage stock levels.
- Admin Order Tracking: Offer an administrative dashboard to view, manage, and track all customer orders, including order status updates and account delivery confirmation.
- AI Product Description Tool: Assist administrators in generating compelling and descriptive text for new streaming service products by utilizing an AI tool to expand on brief inputs.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary accent color: A bold and deep crimson red (#E01424), reminiscent of modern luxury and digital dynamism, creating high contrast against dark backgrounds.
- Background color: A very dark charcoal (#171414) that appears black but has a subtle warmth, providing depth and emphasizing the vibrant primary red.
- Highlight/secondary accent color: A vibrant fuchsia (#F244DC) used sparingly for calls to action, active states, and small details, providing an analogous but striking contrast.
- Headline font: 'Playfair' (serif) for an elegant, high-contrast, and sophisticated touch on titles and headers.
- Body font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for clear readability and a modern, approachable feel in product descriptions and general text.
- Use minimalist, crisp line-art icons that maintain a sleek, modern aesthetic, complementing the bold color scheme.
- Emphasize clean lines and ample negative space to highlight product offerings. Implement a responsive grid system for optimal viewing on various devices.
- Subtle, smooth hover effects on product cards and buttons, with fluid transitions for navigation and modals to enhance the premium user experience.
